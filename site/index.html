AGENTS HAVE AMNESIA

THEY FORGET

Every session starts blank. Six months of learned patterns — customer preferences, escalation rules, edge cases — gone. The agent asks the same questions it already answered last quarter.

THEY CONTRADICT

Support approves the refund. Fraud flags the customer. Neither agent knows what the other decided. The system is coherent on paper and incoherent in practice.

NOTHING IS AUDITABLE

The decision was made. The context is gone. The model weights changed. Good luck explaining to a regulator why your agent approved a transaction at 2am on a Tuesday.
